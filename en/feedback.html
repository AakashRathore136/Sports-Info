Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply click on the Register button, create an account, select your preferred courses, and complete the registration process.

Are the courses free?

We offer both free and premium courses. Basic content is available for free, while advanced modules require a subscription.

Can I learn in my language?

Yes! We support multiple languages including English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Spanish, French, German, and more.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes, upon completing all modules and passing the assessment, you'll receive a digital certificate of completion.
